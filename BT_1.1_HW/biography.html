Biography

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He made his debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008 and quickly established himself as a prolific run-scorer. Known for his aggressive batting style and string leadership, he has set numerous records and chieved great success as the captain of the Indian cricket team.
