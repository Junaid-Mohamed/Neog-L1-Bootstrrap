History

The Taj Mahal was built between 1631 and 1648 and is the result of the profound love Emperor Shah Jahan and for his wife Mumtaz mahal. Mumtaj Mahal died during the child birth. It took over 20,000 workers and srtisans to complete this magnificient structure.

The Taj Mahal is a testament to the srtistry of Mugal architecture and has a rich history, including surviving wars and natural disasters.
