Archtecture

The Taj Mahal is a remarkable blend of variuous styles, including Mugal, Persian and Indian. It is made of white marble, which was brought from different parts of India and Central Asia.

The mausoleum iss known for its symmetrical design, intricate carvings and the iconic central dome.The surrounding gardens and the yamuna river add to its beauty, making it a masterpiece of Islamic archirtrcutre.
